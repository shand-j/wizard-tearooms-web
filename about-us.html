About The Wizard Tearoom

Our location and story

Established in 2016, we are an independent café on this beautiful National Trust site, our building dating back to the 16th century. What started small now runs 7 days a week, 364 days a year, with a unique woodland café to the rear of the building, and a separate ice cream window to the front.

We have a cosy indoor Hire Space, suitable for group gatherings, workshops or corporate hire, and we also have The Meadow – a secure outdoor hire space.

A family business, (Niece + Aunty) we hold friendly service and thoughtful details at the core of what we do. We support independent local businesses who supply us with our lovely produce, which is then put together, on site, by our wonderful team of cooks, bakers + baristas.

We hope you love our place as much as we do.
