Space Hire

Private hire for workshops, events and gatherings

Indoor Space

Our indoor space is available for private hire for a variety of uses:

  • Workshops: Space hired on an unmanaged basis where setup and breakdown is your responsibility
  • Group Gatherings: Part-managed basis with someone on site to assist
  • Corporate Meeting Space: Full or part day hire for business use

Included: Use of all furniture, glassware, crockery and cutlery, log burner and fuel, toilets and small outdoor paddock.

Catering: Self-catering agreeable (no kitchen facilities), or order from our main menu. Plant-based catering available from Root to Flower.

Contact: events@thewizardtearoom.co.uk
